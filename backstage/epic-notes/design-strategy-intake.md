### ü™ú Layer 3 ‚Äî Tier System

**Critical refinement:** Tier ‚â† success. **Tier = how much cognitive energy can be invested.**

**Structural example:**
- **Tier A** ‚Üí high energy
- **Tier B** ‚Üí medium energy
- **Tier C** ‚Üí incubation
- **Tier D** ‚Üí preserved dormancy

**Decision criteria (hybrid):**

- Security
- Liability

**Crucial correction:** Not everything needs to grow. Small projects can have high community currency.

3. What can be automated?
4. What needs human judgment?

**This is adaptive governance.** Not fixed. Periodic review with structural stability.
